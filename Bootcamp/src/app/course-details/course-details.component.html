What You'll Learn

The Experience

BrainStation's full-time Web Development program is tailored to those looking to turn their idea into a startup, make a career switch into technology, or drive their business with digital. It's a challenging yet fun and engaging learning experience that emphasizes collaboration.

By the end of the 10 weeks, you'll have acquired the skills needed to become a Junior Web Developer, as well as a network of like-minded individuals and industry mentors; relationships you'll continue to develop beyond the classroom.
